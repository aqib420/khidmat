Self-Reliance

Orientation & Mobility (O&M)

This program focuses on empowering visually impaired individuals to travel safely, confidently, and independently within their communities using proper white cane techniques and sensory awareness skills.

  • ✓ White Cane Navigation: Techniques for indoor and outdoor urban travel.
  • ✓ Daily Living Skills: Adaptive training for managing personal spaces and cooking safely.

Economic Empowerment

Inclusive Arts & Crafts Workshop

A hands-on, creative skill development module designed for deaf and physically disabled students to manufacture premium hand-crafted items, promoting entrepreneurship and financial independence.

  • ✓ Technical Crafts: Tailoring, weaving, pottery, and tactile arts.
  • ✓ Micro-Business Basics: Basic bookkeeping and pricing items for exhibitions and local craft markets.
