About Our Mission

Bridging the gap for the differently abled community in Pakistan.

Our Story

Empowering Since 2026

EDAP (Empowering the Differently Abled Persons) Pakistan was founded with a clear vision: to ensure that physical or sensory disabilities never stand in the way of professional excellence. The initiative is led by our Founder and Supervisor, Ms. Shazia Hasan, who brings dedicated leadership to the mission of fostering inclusivity.

Operating from the Ida Rieu College for Blind & Deaf in Karachi, Ms. Shazia oversees the project's core mission—providing the specialized tools, vocational training, and psychological support necessary for the blind and deaf community to lead independent, self-reliant lives.

Under her guidance, we are currently focused on building a robust digital presence and automated stakeholder systems to bridge the gap between resources and the individuals who need them most.
